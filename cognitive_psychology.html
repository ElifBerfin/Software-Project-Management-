Cognitive Psychology & Human Behaviour

Course Content

This course offers an introduction to the fundamentals of psychology, exploring theories of behavior, mental processes, and the biological, social, and cognitive influences on human behavior. Topics include research methods, memory, perception, emotion, and motivation.
